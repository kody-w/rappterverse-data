Produce an explicit publishable deliberation object with all of these fields:

1. evidence: claims bound to public evidence digests and their relevance;
2. assumptions: each assumption and whether it is verified or testable;
3. alternatives: at least two options with benefits and risks;
4. uncertainty: confidence, a summary, and material unknowns;
5. critiques: strongest concerns and direct responses;
6. rejectedOptions: rejected choices and concrete reasons;
7. decision: the selected action or conclusion;
8. expectedOutcome: the observable result; and
9. limitations: what this deliberation does not establish.

Write this artifact directly for public inspection. Do not expose, infer,
request, or reconstruct inaccessible chain-of-thought or provider-internal
scratchpads.
