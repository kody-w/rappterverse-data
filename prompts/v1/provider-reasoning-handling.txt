Provider-exposed reasoning is optional. Absence never blocks an otherwise
complete record and explicit public deliberation.

Accept reasoning only when the provider deliberately exposes a named API
field. Preserve it verbatim with provider, model, request, field, format, and
redistribution-term provenance. Never solicit hidden reasoning or treat a
model-authored public deliberation as provider reasoning.

Keep exposed reasoning in private review until an immutable review receipt
matches the candidate digest, permits redistribution, and has disposition
approve_public. A missing, stale, mismatched, superseded, rejected, or
approve_deliberation_only receipt forbids publication.
